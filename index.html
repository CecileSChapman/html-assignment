Education and Career Goals

Hello! First, let me tell you about my post-secondary experience before joining the IMD program. Straight out of high school, I initially accepted an offer for the Mathematics for Education program at York University. My first year of university was a mess. York University went on strike, I went from getting straight A's in high school, to getting C's and D's. After the strike ended, I ended up failing my Calculus II course and I decided that math didn't give me the same joy as it used to. I switched majors to a subject that I love, the Humanities, but it is a very broad subject and hard to start a career with. This is what brought me to the IMD program. I wanted something specific and career-focused, but not another long four years of school. My ultimate goal is to work from home and design websites or something similar.
